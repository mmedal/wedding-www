Matthew & Keilah

For four years, Matthew and Keilah heard all about each other through their mutual friends, Jeff and Jamie (henceforth known as JJ). Matthew had been good friends with Jeff since high school and Keilah became friends with JJ in college. Over this four year period, there were many organized events by JJ to get everyone together. Improbably, one of either Matthew or Keilah could never make it. Keilah even met Matthew's friends before she met Matthew himself. As Matthew and Keilah continued to narrowly miss making each other's acquaintance, JJ began to joke about the prospect of matchmaking the two of them. When Matthew and Keilah were finally introduced, JJ's joke became a wonderful reality.

Moving forwards Together

Immediately following their marriage, Matthew and Keilah will be spending their honeymoon traveling (eating their way) through Paris, Rome, and London. Upon their return home, they are excited to continue building their life together in San Francisco. Matthew and Keilah have dreams of owning a home, adopting a corgi named Pippin, and some day, raising a beautiful family of their own. In the meantime, they'll probably spend time traveling, fine-dining, rock climbing, drinking excellent wine/beer, and spending time with their lovely families.
